What Is The Silver Protocol?

The Silver Protocol is not software. It is not an AI tool. It is a fully managed, human-operated post-sale verification layer that sits between your sales team and your provider relationship.

Every sale your team closes is independently verified by Heimdell Tech AI. We confirm customer identity, record verbal contract acknowledgement, verify Direct Debit authorisation, and produce a timestamped compliance certificate that satisfies provider audit requirements.

The result: Every sale in your pipeline has audit-ready documentation before commission is due. Disputes are resolved with evidence, not arguments. Provider audits become routine verifications, not existential threats.

What the Compliance Certificate Includes

Each Silver Protocol verification produces a provider-ready compliance certificate containing:

👤

Identity Validation

Confirmation that the customer’s identity matches the account holder through independent verification methods.

🎤

Recorded Verbal Confirmation

Timestamped recording of the customer confirming contract terms, service details and agreement to proceed.

🕑

Cooling-Off Acknowledgement

Documented confirmation that the customer was informed of their 14-day cancellation rights under CCR 2013.

💳

Direct Debit Verification

Confirmation of payment authorisation and acknowledgement of the Direct Debit Guarantee under PSR 2017.

📧

SMS & Email Delivery Logs

Timestamped evidence of contract documentation delivery to the customer’s verified contact details.

📄

Certificate Reference

Unique reference number linking the sale to its complete verification record for provider audit requests.

How Deployment Works

Deployment is straightforward. We integrate with your existing sales workflow — whether that’s a CRM, spreadsheet, or manual submission process. Your team submits sales as normal; we handle the verification layer.

Typical deployment takes 5–10 business days from agreement. We handle setup, team briefing, and ongoing verification operations. You receive certificates and a compliance dashboard showing verification status across your pipeline.
