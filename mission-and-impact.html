The RemoteAbility Partnership: The Trust Anchor

RemoteAbility CIC is a Community Interest Company with a statutory Asset Lock, meaning all profits are permanently reinvested into training disabled and neurodivergent adults in AI Operations.

RemoteAbility CIC is not a charity. It is a regulated Community Interest Company — a legal structure that locks all assets to its social mission. No director or shareholder can extract profits. Every pound goes back into training.

What RemoteAbility CIC Does

  • Recruits disabled and neurodivergent adults who want to work in AI operations
  • Delivers a 6-week intensive training programme covering AI tools, CRM management, campaign operations and compliance workflows
  • Deploys trainees into live client campaigns managed by Heimdell Tech AI — not simulations, real work
  • Funds elite operational setups (equipment, software, workspace) through the UK Government’s Access to Work programme

The 6-Week Training Deployment

W1

Week 1–2: Foundations

AI tool proficiency, CRM navigation, data handling, compliance awareness. Trainees learn the systems they will operate independently.

W3

Week 3–4: Supervised Operations

Trainees manage live campaign tasks under direct supervision. Lead qualification, compliance verification, reporting and client communication.

W5

Week 5–6: Independent Deployment

Full operational responsibility on live client accounts. Performance benchmarked against quality standards. Graduates enter the Heimdell Tech AI operations team.

Access to Work: The Operational Advantage

The UK Government’s Access to Work programme provides up to £69,260 per eligible employee per year. This funding covers assistive technology, specialist equipment, support workers, travel costs and workplace adaptations. For Heimdell Tech AI clients, this means every RemoteAbility-trained operator arrives with a fully funded, elite operational setup — at no additional cost to the business.

Access to Work is administered by the Department for Work and Pensions (DWP). Eligibility is assessed individually. Heimdell Tech AI and RemoteAbility CIC assist with application and evidence submission.
