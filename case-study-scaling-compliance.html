The Challenge

This Sky reseller was on an aggressive growth trajectory. In three months, they went from 30 field agents generating around 100 sales per month to 60 agents pushing past 200 monthly sales.

The growth was excellent for revenue — but it created a serious operational problem:

  • Compliance verification was a manual process handled by one back-office team member
  • At 100 sales/month, that person could just about keep up. At 200+, it was impossible
  • Unverified sales were piling up — by the time verification caught up, the cooling-off period had often expired
  • Clawbacks were climbing from £800/month to £2,600/month as volume outpaced verification
  • Hiring a second compliance person would cost £28,000–£32,000 per year — and wouldn’t scale either

The agency director needed a compliance solution that could scale with the business without adding fixed headcount costs.

The Solution: The Silver Protocol

The Silver Protocol was deployed as a fully outsourced, managed compliance verification layer. Heimdell Tech AI took over the entire verification process — the agency’s in-house compliance person was reassigned to quality management.

What Was Implemented

  • Scalable verification pipeline — every sale entered the queue automatically via CRM integration
  • Post-sale verification calls completed within 24 hours of each sign-up
  • Compliance certificates generated for every verified sale
  • Real-time dashboard showing sales status, agent performance, and verification metrics
  • Automatic escalation for high-risk sales (e.g., vulnerable customers, incomplete records)

The systems architecture was designed to handle unlimited volume. Whether the agency processed 200 or 2,000 sales per month, the verification process remained the same — fully managed by Heimdell Tech AI with no additional work for the reseller.

Scaling Timeline
