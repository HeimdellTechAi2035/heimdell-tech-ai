Commissions lost to process breakdowns, disputes that should have been prevented, and a business that gets harder to manage every month.

Common Engagements

Common engagements include CRM buildout (4–6 weeks), compliance infrastructure setup (2–4 weeks), workflow optimisation (ongoing retainer), and full fractional ops (ongoing support). Each is scoped to your specific requirements with clear deliverables and timeline.

💼 Agency CRM Overhaul

Your CRM is a dumping ground of leads, statuses and notes with no structure. We redesign your pipeline, automate stage transitions, add compliance checkpoints and create dashboards that show you exactly where every sale stands.

Outcome: 60% reduction in manual data entry, real-time pipeline visibility, zero missed compliance steps.

📈 Commission Reconciliation System

You’re manually cross-referencing provider statements with your sales records every month. We build an automated reconciliation workflow that flags discrepancies, tracks clawbacks and produces exception reports automatically.

Outcome: Commission reconciliation reduced from 3 days to 30 minutes. Clawback patterns identified automatically.

👥 New Team Onboarding System

Every new agent gets a different training experience depending on who’s available that day. We create structured onboarding workflows, role-specific training paths and competency checkpoints that produce consistent, compliant agents.

Outcome: Agent ramp-up time cut in half. Compliance error rate among new starters drops to near zero.

⚖ Provider Audit Preparation

A provider audit request has arrived and you’re scrambling. We establish standing audit-readiness processes — pre-built export templates, evidence bundles and a documentation structure that means audit responses take hours, not weeks.

Outcome: Audit response time reduced from weeks to same-day. Zero evidence gaps.
