Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions About Commission Leakage

What causes commission clawbacks for UK telecom resellers?

+
Missing compliance evidence that providers require for commission release. Clawbacks occur when providers dispute sale validity due to missing cooling-off confirmation under Consumer Contracts Regulations 2013, unverified customer identity, absent contract acknowledgements, or failed Direct Debit mandates under PSR 2017.

How much commission do telecom resellers lose to compliance gaps?

+
£2,000–£5,000 per month for resellers processing 100+ sales. Common causes include cooling-off disputes, Direct Debit indemnity claims, and failed provider audits. The Financial Leakage Audit calculates your specific exposure based on your current post-sale process.

What is a Financial Leakage Audit?

+
A free 15-minute diagnostic call that identifies your compliance gaps. We examine your post-sale workflow against provider compliance requirements: sale validation, Direct Debit handling, contract confirmation, and provider audit readiness. You receive an estimated monthly financial impact and prioritised action plan.

How do I prepare for a provider compliance audit?

+
You need timestamped evidence for every sale: customer consent, cooling-off notification, identity verification, and Direct Debit mandate confirmation. A Financial Leakage Audit assesses your current documentation completeness and identifies gaps before the provider does — preventing clawbacks rather than reacting to them.
