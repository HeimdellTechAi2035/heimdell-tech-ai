The reality: Every sale that goes through without a verified identity check, a recorded contract confirmation or a timestamped audit trail is a sale that can be clawed back. The 15-Minute Financial Leakage Audit shows you exactly which of your sales are exposed — and what the total cost is. What the Audit Covers

In just 15 minutes, we conduct a structured diagnostic of your current post-sale workflow, examining the four areas where financial leakage typically originates:

🔍

Sale Validation Process

How are you confirming customer identity at the point of sale? We check whether your current method would survive a provider compliance audit.

💳

Direct Debit Mandate Handling

Are your mandate confirmations compliant with PSR 2017? We identify exposure to “non-consent” disputes and indemnity claims.

📝

Contract Confirmation Workflow

Do you have a recorded, timestamped confirmation of every contract? We review whether your evidence would hold in a dispute scenario.

📈

Provider Audit Readiness

If your provider demanded compliance evidence for the last 50 sales tomorrow, could you produce it? We assess your audit trail completeness.
