The Challenge

This Virgin Media reseller operated a 150-agent sales operation across the North West of England. With hundreds of door-to-door and field sales completed each month, their back-office team struggled to verify every sale before the cooling-off period expired.

The consequences were severe:

  • £4,200 per month in commission clawbacks from unverified or disputed sign-ups
  • Cooling-off cancellations that were only discovered after commission had been paid out
  • No centralised record of which sales had been properly verified
  • Growing risk of a Virgin Media compliance audit flag due to inconsistent documentation

The agency owner knew the problem was costing them heavily, but with 150 agents generating sales daily, manual verification was impossible to scale. They needed a system that could verify every sale without adding headcount.

The Solution: The Silver Protocol

The Silver Protocol was deployed as a fully managed, human-operated post-sale compliance verification layer. Heimdell Tech AI’s team handled the entire process — no additional staff were hired by the reseller.

What Was Implemented

  • Post-sale verification calls to every customer within 24 hours of sign-up
  • Compliance certificates generated and stored for each verified sale
  • Real-time dashboard showing verification status for every agent and sale
  • Escalation triggers for high-risk sales identified during verification
  • Provider audit evidence packs available on demand

The entire systems architecture was built and managed by Heimdell Tech AI. The reseller’s sales team continued operating exactly as before — the protocol ran invisibly behind their existing workflow.

Implementation Timeline

W1

Week 1: Free Leakage Audit

A 15-minute Financial Leakage Audit identified £4,200/month in preventable clawbacks. Root causes: 23% of sales had no post-sale verification, 11% had incomplete customer records.

W2

Week 2–3: Protocol Deployment

Verification scripts, compliance certificate templates, and the real-time dashboard were built and tested. Integration with the existing CRM was completed in 4 working days.

W4

Week 4: Live Operations Begin

All new sales entered the verification pipeline. Heimdell Tech AI operators began verifying each sale within 24 hours.

W12

Week 12: Full Results Confirmed

After a full 90-day cycle, clawbacks had dropped from £4,200/month to £252/month — a 94% reduction. The remaining 6% were genuine customer cancellations unrelated to compliance gaps.
